Dragon Fruit Delight

How to Cook Dragon Fruit Delight

Welcome to the recipe for Dragon Fruit Delight, a delicious and exotic dessert that will impress your friends and family. Follow these simple steps to create a delightful treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 Dragon Fruits
  • 1 cup of Greek Yogurt
  • 1/4 cup of Honey
  • 1/2 cup of Granola
  • Fresh Mint Leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cut the dragon fruits in half and scoop out the flesh using a spoon. Set the shells aside for serving.
  2. In a bowl, mix the dragon fruit flesh with Greek yogurt and honey until well combined.
  3. Spoon the mixture back into the dragon fruit shells.
  4. Top with granola and garnish with fresh mint leaves.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your Dragon Fruit Delight!
